Emma Watson

English Actress/Model/Activist

British actress and activist, Emma Watson, is well-known for playing ithe pivotal character of Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter movie series. She has achieved popularity beyond the screen and is now a well-known supporter of women's rights.
As a result of Watson's ardent campaigning, the HeForShe campaign was started, she was named a UN Women Goodwill Ambassador, and she spoke passionately at the UN about the value of including men in the battle for gender equality.
Numerous women all around the world have been motivated by her advocacy to take action, speak out, and fight for chances and rights that are equal.

Emma Watson: Acting Break

The British Actress reveals whether she would ever return to acting after four-year break

“I wasn’t very happy if I’m being honest. I think I felt a bit caged,” she said in an interview with the Financial Times. “The thing I found really hard was that I had to go out and sell something that I really didn’t have very much control over.
She added: “It was very difficult to have to be the face and the spokesperson for things where I didn’t get to be involved in the process.”
That does not mean to say she will never step in front of a camera again. Watson said she would be open to the idea if the role was “right”.
She explained she did not want to “fracture herself into different faces and people”, adding: “I don’t want to switch into robot mode any more.”
